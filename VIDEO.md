# 📽️ MentorAI Hackathon Submission Video Script
> **Length:** ~2 Minutes  
> **Speaker:** Software Engineer / Founder  
> **Tone:** Highly Professional, Decisive, and Metrics-Driven

---

## 🎬 Part 1: Visual Setup and Recording Guidelines

### Recording Preparation Checklist
1. **Resolution**: Record at 1080p, widescreen (16:9 ratio), with high-quality browser rendering.
2. **Setup**: Have both **Immersive Dark Theme** and **High-Contrast Light Theme** tabs configured.
3. **Whiteboard Image**: Save a whiteboard mockup image or UML diagram blueprint on your desktop (e.g., a simple chart with database nodes and client lines) ready to upload.
4. **Volume**: Ensure microphone audio is crisp and browser/speech synthesis output volume is enabled.
